Product Privacy Statement Viren

General information

Your privacy is important to us.

This privacy statement explains what data Teal Partners BV may collect through our product called "Viren" and how we use that data ("Product Privacy Statement"). Viren is a licence based tool that provides features for service management and remote collaboration.

Teal Partners is a private limited liability company incorporated under Belgian law, having its offices at Damplein 23, 2060 Belgium, and registered with the Crossroads Bank for Enterprises under number 0642.824.542 ("Teal Partners", "we", "our", "us").

This Product Privacy Statement can be modified, e.g. as a result of new functionalities of Viren. We thus advise you to check this Product Privacy Statement regularly.

When does this Product Privacy Statement apply?

This Product Privacy Statement applies to processing of personal data through Viren.

This Product Privacy Statement does not apply to other processing activities by Teal Partners which are not related to Viren. In such cases the general Privacy Policy available on our website will apply.

Who is responsible for the processing of your personal data?

Viren is intended for use by organizations ("end users") and is administered and controlled by these end users.

When your organization, as an end user, is using Viren, your organization is responsible for the processing of these data through Viren and is hence the data controller. This means that your organization determines for what purposes and with what means the data are processed. If you as a data subject wish to exercise your rights as described under the last section of this Product Privacy Statement, you will have to address the end user in the first place. In this case Teal Partners will not have the right to directly assist you.

Teal Partners as a processor

Teal Partners may be processing personal data on behalf of end users. In those cases Teal Partners acts as a data processor and a data processing agreement has been agreed upon.

Teal Partners as a controller

In addition to the above, Teal Partners also processes personal data on its own initiative for the purposes explained below, without receiving instructions from the end user. With regard to these processing activities, Teal Partners itself is responsible for processing, and therefore data controller next to the processing activities by the end user. Teal Partners only processes the personal data in limited cases. All these cases are explained in this Policy.

Which data may Teal Partners process about you?

The data Teal Partners collects as a data controller depend on the context of interactions with Teal Partners.

We collect data in two ways:

Information provided by you

  • Contact data: when you contact Teal Partners to make use of Viren or for customer support or when you provide information to us, we collect your first and last name, language, email address, your correspondence with Teal Partners, and/or other personal and/or contact details;
  • Login data: when you login to use Viren, we collect your user name and password, your email address, browser version, operating system version and your IP address;
  • Support data: when you engage Teal Partners for support and maintenance, we collect data about you, telephone conversations or chat/mail sessions, any problems you experience with them and other details related to an error, incident or bug. Error reports can include data such as the type or severity of the problem, details of the software related to an error, contact or authentication data, log files, usernames, the content of your communications with Teal Partners, and system and registry data about software installations.

Automatically obtained information

  • Product usage data: data about the usage of Viren, data about the used features, the selected settings, the software configurations and performance data which helps us to diagnose problems in the tool, and to improve Viren and provide solutions;
  • Device, connectivity and configuration data such as browser version, operating system version, unique device identifiers like IP address ...

For what purposes do we use personal data?

We use personal data for the following purposes:

  • In order to activate access to Viren:

    We may collect contact details about the individual user and his/her organization as it is entered in the Teal Partners activation registration portal.

    Upon completion of the registration process by the person performing the installation, the person who has been identified as end user administrator receives an email based on the user identification data provided, which allows the end user administrator to create an account.

    The data is processed on the basis of the performance of a contract.

  • In order to use the service:

    The end user administrator can decide which individuals have access to use Viren.

    The data is processed on the basis of the performance of a contract.

  • In order to provide support:

    We may collect user identification and environmental data as part of debug, info, error logs of user interactions with the Viren portal. All Viren components generate logs that are used to monitor, operate and provide support for the service.

    The data is processed on the basis of the performance of a contract.

  • In order to improve the product:

    User identification and environmental data is collected as part of debug, info, error logs of user interactions with the Viren portal which is aggregated in order to provide statistics, analytics and insight to the users of Viren and improve the product.

    This data is processed on the basis of Teal Partners' legitimate interest to continually improve its products.

Who do we share data with?

Teal Partners is selective in the parties to whom it discloses data and chooses them carefully. In all cases, we will ensure that sufficient safeguards for the protection and confidentiality of the data are in place. When we provide data to third parties, they are not permitted to use the data for any reason outside of the scope for which we contracted them.

Teal Partners may share the personal data with third parties to perform certain (processing) activities:

  • cloud service providers to perform certain (processing) activities (Microsoft Azure Services, SendGrid, CloudFlare);
  • business partners who assist us in protecting and securing the Teal Partners products and systems;
  • in certain cases Teal Partners may transfer your personal data to attorneys and external advisors if this is necessary to give Teal Partners advice or to defend our rights;
  • supervisory authorities in case of a lawful request;
  • with other companies of the SD Worx group, which Teal Partners is part of;
  • possible acquirers of the whole or part of Teal Partners in the event that (a part of) our activities would be taken over by a third party.

In addition to the above, Teal Partners reserves the right to disclose personal data to other parties under the following conditions: (i) when required by law or a court order; (ii) when trying to protect against or prevent actual or potential fraud or unauthorized transactions; or (iii) when investigating suspected fraud which has already taken place.

How long do we keep the data?

Teal Partners will retain your personal data in a form which permits identification for no longer than needed

  • for the purposes for which it was collected;
  • for the term of the contract; or
  • as necessary to comply with regulatory, legal, audit or internal compliance requirements, to resolve disputes, and to enforce our agreements.

Where do we process personal data?

Your personal data will be kept in Belgium, another country within the European Economic Area or a destination country which, according to the European Commission, offers an adequate level of protection. In the event of a transfer of data to a destination country that does not in itself offer an adequate level of protection according to the European Commission, Teal Partners will agree with the party in question on the necessary contractual safeguards by concluding a European Commission's model data protection contract (so-called standard contractual clauses).

How are your personal data protected?

Teal Partners strives to protect the data by means of technical an organizational measures, such as firewalls and secured servers that may only be accessed by a limited number of people holding special access rights and with respect to the nature of the data.

What are your rights and how can you exercise them?

You have a number of rights with regard to your personal data, such as the right of access, the right to rectification of inaccurate personal data, the right to erasure or restriction of processing, the right to data portability, the right not to be subject to profiling and the right to object to the processing. Some of these rights have a very specific scope or are subject to special conditions or exceptions. Please contact our Privacy Team for any questions or requests regarding the processing of your personal data. Any request to exercise a relevant right must be duly signed and dated. Teal Partners cannot process your request without proof of your identity. On grounds relating to your particular situation, you also have the right to object at any time to the processing of your personal data on the basis of the legitimate interest of Teal Partners acting as a controller. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the supervisory authority of your habitual residence, your place of work or the place of an alleged infringement. The Belgian Data Protection Authority is the supervisory authority for Belgium and can be contacted at contact@apd-gba.be.
